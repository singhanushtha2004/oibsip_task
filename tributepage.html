A Tribute to Jhanshi ki Rani

Why I Admire

Rani Lakshmi Bai is admired for her courage, leadership, and unwavering spirit in India's struggle against British rule. As the Queen of Jhansi, she led her army with fierce determination, defying oppression and inspiring generations. Her bravery in battle and sacrifice for freedom make her a timeless symbol of resistance.

Achievements & Contributions

  • Leadership in the 1857 Revolt
  • Battle of Kotah-ki-Serai
  • Symbol of Resistance
